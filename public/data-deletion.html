Data Deletion Instructions for Save The Plate

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data from Save The Plate, please follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website at https://savetheplate.tn/data-deletion
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Submit the deletion request form

Alternative Method

You can also send an email to savetheplatetunisia@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Any account identifiers you have

Processing Time

We will process your data deletion request within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information
  • Profile data
  • Order history
  • Any content you have posted
